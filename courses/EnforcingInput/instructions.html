Looping Practice: Enforcing Inputs

Another use for while loops is to ask a user question with a limited set of answers. Remember that a while loop works similar to an if statement.

For example, it is reasonable that when we ask someone the year they were born, that they were born after the year 1900. If they enter a year before that, we will ask them again - and keep asking them until they give us an answer we expect.
